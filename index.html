Crypto Networks VPN

Our product is a VPN service that allows users to establish an anonymous high-speed connection through one of our servers in EU using the VLESS/Reality protocol. The service's key feature is payments processing in a cryptocurrency of your choice (though we encourage the use of XMR – Monero), which is fully consistent with our mission:

We strive to provide every Internet user with complete freedom of information

In doing so, we are guided by the following values:

Privacy

DeFi

Anonymity
Our VPN service is fully committed to protecting user data and maintaining user confidentiality: no traffic, timestamp, DNS, bandwidth, or IP logging. Only user count, offline/online status and subscription status are available to our administrators and support agents.
